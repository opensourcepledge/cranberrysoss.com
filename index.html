All Proceeds Go to Open Source Maintainers

Every penny made from sOSS sales goes towards supporting the Open Source maintainers we all depend on.

But which maintainers? The Open Source ecosystem is complex, and keystone maintainers are difficult to identify.

That's where thanks.dev comes in, who have used their dependency analysis model to create a weighted list of the 1000 most depended upon Open Source maintainers globally.

No model is perfect, and we certainly haven't been able to include all keystone maintainers. But we think the distribution thanks.dev came up with makes a lot of sense. And you don't have to take our word for it. In the spirit of transparency, our funding distribution is public.

FAQs

Is this real?

Yes.

Isn’t this kinda stupid? There are easier, cheaper ways to get cranberry sauce.

No, not kinda stupid. VERY stupid. But…would you be paying attention if it weren’t? The sustainability crisis in OSS — and maintainers in general — need more attention, and more money to support them. Hopefully, Cranberry sOSS provides both.

Do maintainers need our help, really?

Yes, really. In fact, a recent report by University of Edinburgh PhD researcher Miranda Heath outlined the many challenges maintainers face, which often lead to burnout and global security vulnerabilities.
